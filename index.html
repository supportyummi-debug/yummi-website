Meet Ashraf

A man like millions living away from home, missing his mother's homemade food after a long day. Restaurants are everywhere, but the warmth of a home-cooked meal is hard to find. That's why I created YUMMI—an app that connects licensed home cooks with people craving authentic homemade meals across Qatar.

👋

I'm Hamid

A Grade 9 student and founder of YUMMI. I created YUMMI to solve a simple problem: thousands of people in Qatar miss the comfort of homemade food. My goal is to support licensed home cooks while giving students, bachelors, and families easy access to fresh, authentic meals.

FAQ

Are the cooks licensed? 🔽

Yes! Safety and quality are our top priorities. All home cooks on YUMMI must hold a valid, official cooking license in Qatar before they can join the platform and share their meals.
